About Me

I'm Claudio, a data professional with a deep passion for uncovering insights hidden within data. My journey into the world of data started while watching NBA games, where I became fascinated by how teams used statistics to shape strategies and gain a competitive advantage. Over the years, I've gained strong technical skills in Python, SQL, and data visualisation tools like Power BI and Tableau, creating dashboards and optimising processes to increase efficiency and deliver impactful results.

My professional experience extend over roles in multinational and tech-driven companies, where I collaborated with cross-functional teams, streamlined workflows, and improved data quality to support strategic objectives.
